About Us

We believe China deserves to be experienced,
not just visited.

China is vast, layered, and often misunderstood — not because it is hard to love, but because it can be hard to enter without the right support.

At Orient Surprises Travel, we design private China journeys for international guests who want more than a standard itinerary. We combine local knowledge, cultural understanding, practical planning, and real-time support to help travelers experience China with comfort, clarity, and depth.

Many of our team members have studied, worked, or built lives abroad. We understand both Chinese travel operations and the expectations of international guests. That perspective shapes the way we plan every journey.

From the first conversation, guests work with a dedicated travel consultant who listens to how they travel, what matters to them, and what kind of China experience they hope to have. Then we design a journey around their pace, interests, budget, comfort level, and special needs.

We do not compete with mass-market travel agencies on price. We focus on thoughtful, personalized travel that respects our guests' time, comfort, and curiosity.

"China has been sharing its stories for five thousand years. We are here to make sure yours begins on the right chapter."

What We Arrange

Everything Your China Journey Needs

From the first airport pickup to the final drop-off, we handle the logistics so you can focus on the experience.

Domestic & International Flights

We mainly arrange domestic flights within China and can assist with international flight bookings when needed. Our team provides flight suggestions, airport transfer coordination, itinerary connection planning, and change assistance.

Private Driver & Vehicle Service

We arrange comfortable private vehicles including 5-seat cars, 7-seat business vans, minibuses, and coaches. Services include airport transfers, city transportation, and intercity travel arrangements. English communication is supported by guides or travel consultants.

English-Speaking & Multilingual Guides

We arrange professional English-speaking guides across China. Other languages such as Arabic, Spanish, French, Russian, and more can be arranged upon request depending on destination and travel dates.

Hotel Booking

We usually recommend comfortable 4-star hotels for a balanced travel experience. 3-star and 5-star hotels can also be arranged according to budget, location, comfort preference, and travel style.

Attraction Tickets & Reservations

We assist with ticket booking and reservations for major attractions across China, helping travelers save time and avoid confusion when visiting popular landmarks, museums, scenic areas, shows, cruises, and cultural experiences.

Tailor-Made Itinerary Planning

Every itinerary is designed around the guest's travel style. We consider budget, age, fitness level, travel pace, dietary restrictions, religious needs, shopping interests, photography goals, family requirements, and special preferences.

Travel Preparation Guidance

We provide practical travel preparation guidance to help guests understand what to expect before and during their China journey. From visa questions to payment tips, we help you arrive prepared and confident.

How We Work

How We Take Care of Your Journey

From the first conversation to your final airport drop-off, our team manages the details behind the scenes so your trip feels smooth, safe, and personal.

01

We Understand Your Travel Needs

Before designing your itinerary, we first learn about your group size, travel dates, budget, preferred cities, hotel standards, dietary restrictions, fitness level, religious needs, and special interests.

02

We Design a Personalized Plan

Depending on the complexity of the trip, we usually prepare the first itinerary proposal within 24 hours to 1–2 working days. Each plan is designed around your pace, comfort level, and travel style.

03

We Arrange the Details

Once the itinerary is confirmed, our team helps arrange hotels, attraction tickets, English-speaking guides, private drivers and vehicles, domestic flights, high-speed train tickets, restaurants, shows, and local experiences.

04

We Support You During the Trip

After guests arrive in China, we provide dedicated group communication, daily reminders, driver and guide coordination, translation support, and real-time assistance for unexpected situations.

05

We Help When Plans Change

Our team can assist with flight delays, lost luggage, lost documents, medical visits, getting lost, communication difficulties, long attraction queues, weather changes, and other on-trip issues.

Emergency Assistance

Backup Planning

For selected itineraries, we prepare backup suggestions for rainy days, long queues, senior travelers, children, and guests who prefer a slower travel pace.

🛡️

Optional Travel Accident Insurance

Travel accident insurance is not mandatory. Guests may choose to purchase it after arrival in China, estimated at around 100 CNY per person. We also recommend international guests prepare their own overseas travel insurance before departure.
